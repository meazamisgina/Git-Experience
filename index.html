My Two Weeks at AkiraChix

Hello, my name is Meaza Misgina and I am from Tigray, Ethiopia. I am currently learning at the AkiraChix CodeHive program. It has been almost three weeks since I joined the program and I'm here to share with you my experience during these weeks; and I am really grateful to the AkiraChix community for allowing me to join the tech world by believing in my abilities.

Good Experiences


  1. Diversity And Inclusion

    AkiraChix respects and celebrates diversity, especially in terms of religion and food options. This inclusive environment makes everyone feel valued and supported.

  2. Empowerment Through Communication

    The program encourages students to express themselves confidently, encouraging a culture of self-expression and assertiveness.

  3. Collaborative Learning Approach

    The emphasis on teamwork and collaboration helps us (students)learn how to work effectively with others, which is crucial in the tech industry.

  4. Welcoming Environment

    The warm welcome and ongoing support provided to new students make the transition smoother and more enjoyable.

  5. Organized Program Structure

    The program is well-organized, with clear roles and responsibilities among staff members, ensuring that everything runs smoothly.

  6. Effective Communication Among Trainers

    Trainers work together seamlessly to provide comprehensive learning experiences, ensuring that students receive a thorough understanding of the topics.

Not So Good Experiences


  • Limited Practice Time After Class

    After classes end, we have limited time to stay in the lab/classroom to practice on our own. While this encourages us to use our time efficiently, it can be challenging until we adapt to managing our time effectively.

  • Time Management Challenges

    Managing time effectively to meet project deadlines can be difficult, especially when we're still adjusting to the program's pace. It requires discipline and planning, which can be stressful until we get into a rhythm
